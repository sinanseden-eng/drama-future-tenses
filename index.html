Teacher Collaboration Draft

Future-Vision Talk Show

A customizable Green Screen drama script to introduce Future Continuous and Future Perfect tenses!

📝 How to use this template

Each participating teacher should fill in the pink brackets [like this] with their own subject-specific jokes, exaggerations, and inside jokes.

Make sure to clearly emphasize the bright blue grammar structures when speaking (e.g., will have exploded).

🎭 Characters

  • 🎙️
    The Host Needs ridiculous props (like a whisk microphone) and giant sunglasses. Mood/Personality: Over-the-top, cheesy game-show announcer. High energy, loud, and constantly smiling.
  • 👩‍🏫
    Teacher 1: The Overworked Survivor Mood/Personality: Frantic, heavily caffeinated, and panicked. Speaks rapidly, wide-eyed, and constantly checks their watch.
  • 👨‍🏫
    Teacher 2: The Dramatic Visionary Mood/Personality: Intense, highly theatrical, and overly serious. Treats teaching like an epic sci-fi movie. Uses grand hand gestures.
  • 🧑‍🏫
    Teacher 3: The Checked-Out Rebel Mood/Personality: Sleepy, entirely unbothered, or "too cool." Counting down the seconds to summer, totally relaxed, slouching.
  • 🥽
    Teacher 4: The Mad Scientist Mood/Personality: Wild, chaotic, and explosive. Speaks in loud bursts, laughs maniacally at their own plans, and wears imaginary safety goggles.
  • 🧘
    Teacher 5: The Zen Guru Mood/Personality: Painfully slow, incredibly peaceful. Speaks in a low, soothing whisper while doing yoga poses or holding invisible crystals.
  • 🕵️
    Teacher 6: The Conspiracy Theorist Mood/Personality: Paranoid, looking around suspiciously. Whispers loudly, trusts no one, and acts like everything is a giant secret.

💡 Brainstorming Cheat Sheet

Future Perfect

will have + past participle

Focus on the result or what will be completely finished by a deadline.

Ideas to steal:
  • will have graded a million papers
  • will have lost my voice
  • will have read every book in the library
  • will have eaten 500 cafeteria pizzas

Future Continuous

will be + verb-ing

Focus on the action in progress at a specific time in the future.

Ideas to steal:
  • will be crying over test scores
  • will be training ninja squirrels
  • will be sleeping peacefully
  • will be grading while skydiving
